About Us

Our History

Founded in 2020, our dealership began with a vision to provide exceptional automotive experiences in Indonesia. Initially a small, family-run enterprise, we quickly gained a reputation for our personalized customer service and quality vehicle offerings. Over the years, we have expanded our inventory and services, becoming a trusted name in the community for both new and pre-owned cars.
